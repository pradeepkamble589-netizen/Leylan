Basic Information

Scientific Name
Cupressus × leylandii
Family
Cupressaceae
Type
Evergreen Conifer Tree
Origin
Hybrid developed in United Kingdom
Average Height
60 – 70 feet
Growth Rate
Very Fast Growing

About the Tree

The Leyland Cypress is a fast-growing evergreen tree widely used as a privacy hedge or windbreak. It is a hybrid between the Monterey Cypress and the Nootka Cypress. Because of its rapid growth and dense foliage, it is popular in gardens, parks, and landscapes.

Characteristics

  • Soft green needle-like leaves
  • Dense foliage providing excellent privacy
  • Adaptable to different soil types
  • Resistant to wind and pollution
  • Grows very quickly compared to many other trees

Benefits

  • 🌿 Provides natural privacy hedge
  • 🌬️ Acts as windbreak barrier
  • 🌳 Improves landscape beauty
  • 🌎 Helps improve air quality
  • 🐦 Provides shelter for birds and wildlife

Interesting Facts

  • One of the fastest growing evergreen trees.
  • Can grow up to 3–4 feet per year.
  • Often used around homes for natural fencing.
  • Popular tree for landscaping projects.
